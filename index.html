About Me

American journalist reporting from Ukraine, providing on-air commentary for CNN and France 24, and writing for leading publications including The Free Press, Atlantic Council, Wall Street Journal, Kyiv Independent, and National Review.

Based in Ukraine, I cover international affairs and foreign policy, with a focus on the ongoing conflict and its broader implications. My work combines on-the-ground reporting with analytical commentary, contributing to Pulse, a Substack newsletter on foreign affairs from Ukraine.
